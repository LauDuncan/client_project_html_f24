Welcome to CEW+

Supporting women, underserved communities, and nontraditional students at the University of Michigan.

CEW+ offers career development, counseling, financial aid, and leadership programs to foster professional and academic growth. Here's an overview of the resources available:

  1. Career Counseling
  2. Scholarships and Fellowships
  3. Workshops and Programs
  4. Access by Appointment
  5. Additional Resources and Advocacy

Services Provided

CEW+ provides a variety of services to support nontraditional and underserved students:

Career Counseling

One-on-one career and education counseling for nontraditional students, including personalized guidance.

Scholarships and Fellowships

Financial aid for returning students, students with caregiving responsibilities, and other nontraditional learners.

Workshops and Programs

Educational and professional development workshops focused on academic and career growth.

Accessing CEW+

Here's how to access CEW+ services:

Location

Located centrally on the University of Michigan campus and open to all students and staff.

Appointments

Offers individual counseling sessions and career consultations by appointment.

Eligibility

Primarily serves underserved communities and nontraditional students, but services are available to all.

Additional Resources

CEW+ also provides the following additional resources:

Events and Networking

Hosts regular events and networking opportunities, such as leadership workshops and career development sessions.

Advocacy and Research

CEW+ conducts research and advocates for policies that benefit nontraditional students and underserved communities.

Volunteer Opportunities

Engage in community service and volunteerism through CEW+ programs.
