CAPS

Welcome to CAPS

Counseling and Psychological Services (CAPS) provides mental health support to University of Michigan students.

CAPS offers a range of services to promote student mental wellness and success. Here's an overview of the available resources:

  1. Individual Counseling
  2. Group Therapy
  3. Crisis Support
  4. Workshops and Programs
  5. Additional Resources and Peer Support

Services Provided

CAPS provides a variety of services to support mental health and well-being:

Individual Counseling

One-on-one counseling sessions to help students navigate personal, academic, and mental health challenges.

Group Therapy

Facilitated group sessions where students can share experiences and develop coping strategies together.

Crisis Support

Immediate mental health support for students in urgent situations, available both in-person and virtually.

Workshops and Programs

Workshops focused on topics like stress management, anxiety, relationships, and self-care techniques.

Accessing CAPS

Here's how to access CAPS services:

Location

Located on central campus, with virtual consultation options available for all students.

Appointments

Appointments can be booked online, with same-day or emergency walk-in services available for crisis situations.

Eligibility

Free services available to all currently enrolled University of Michigan students.

Additional Resources

CAPS offers extra resources to support student mental health:

Wellness Coaching

Helps students develop personalized strategies for managing stress, time, and overall well-being.

Peer Counseling

Provides students with the option to talk with trained peers for additional support.

Self-Help Tools

Access to mindfulness exercises, mental health apps, and other online tools for self-guided mental health management.
